Common Questions

What happens after the 7-day trial?

You'll be able to continue with the SaaS plan once available, or your account will pause until you're ready to subscribe. No data loss.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All business data is encrypted and never shared. We don't use your data for AI training.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No long-term contracts. Cancel with one email to support@mailminer.work.

Can team members share one account?

Yes. Team members share storage space, but each person has an independent experience based on their email address.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal. Subscribe easily via our PayPal link.
