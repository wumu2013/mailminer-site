Solutions for Every Trade Workflow

From first inquiry to final delivery, MailMiner handles the details so you can focus on building relationships.

🏭 Supplier Communication

Managing multiple suppliers means endless email threads. MailMiner helps you:

  • Auto-reply common supplier questions with your preferred responses
  • Compare quotes side-by-side from different suppliers
  • Track order status and delivery timelines automatically
  • Flag price changes or unusual terms for your review

Result: Faster response times, fewer miscommunications, more time for negotiation.

📝 Contract Review

Every contract sent to overseas partners gets checked automatically. MailMiner:

  • Reviews payment terms and flags unusual clauses
  • Checks compliance with your company's trade policies
  • Highlights missing or risky terms you may have missed
  • Prepares a summary so you can review in seconds, not hours

Result: Fewer costly mistakes, stronger partnerships, peace of mind.

💳 Payment Processing

Invoice and payment workflows handled through email. MailMiner:

  • Validates invoice amounts against purchase orders
  • Checks for duplicate or suspicious invoices
  • Routes approvals to the right people automatically
  • Tracks payment status and sends reminders as needed

Result: Faster processing, fewer errors, better cash flow management.

📦 Documentation

Export and import documents managed efficiently. MailMiner:

  • Prepares draft documents for your review and signature
  • Tracks required documents across shipments
  • Reminds you of upcoming renewals or expiring licenses
  • Keeps a searchable archive of all trade documents

Result: Less paperwork, fewer delays, organized records.
